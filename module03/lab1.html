Every entry level 3D printing enthusiest should learn is the diffenfernce between plug in and play 3d printers vs assembly required printers. Plug in plays are usally the eassiest way the enjoy the hobby of 3d printing. Plug in and play 3d printers come already assembled with minor amounts of intstructions. Assembly required printers will provide tools to build your printer out of the box with instrution guides. There will be time when the instrutions of the assembly 3d printers are non exsistent and online videos are required for assembly. When selecting a 3d printer look at the pros and cons of both side and choose according to the users preference and quality of the prints.
